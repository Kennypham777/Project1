Blackjack Rules

1. Card Values:

- 2-10 = Face value.

- Jack, Queen, King = 10 points.

- Ace = 1 or 11 points (whichever benefits the hand).

2. Goal:

- Get as close to 21 as possible without going over (busting).

- Beat the dealer’s hand without busting.

3. Initial Deal:

- You get two cards face up.

- Dealer gets two cards (one face up, one face down).

4. Blackjack:

- If you get an Ace + 10-value card = Blackjack!

- Blackjack beats all hands except the dealer's Blackjack.

5. Player’s Choices:

- Hit: Take another card.

- Stand: Keep your current hand.

- Double Down: Double your bet, take one more card, and then stand.

- Split: If your first two cards are a pair, split them into two separate hands.

6. Dealer’s Rules:

- Dealer must hit until their hand totals 17 or more.

- Dealer stands on 17 or higher (in some games, they hit on soft 17).

7. Winning:

- If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's, you win.

- If the dealer busts (goes over 21), you win.

- If both hands are the same, it's a tie (push).

8. Payouts:

- Regular win = 1:1 (you win the same as your bet).

- Blackjack = 3:2 payout.

- Push = No win or loss (you get your bet back).
